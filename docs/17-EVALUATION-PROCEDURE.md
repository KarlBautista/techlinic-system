Evaluation Procedure

Based on the result of the evaluation conducted, the "TechClinic: A Health Record and Analytics System for Technological University of the Philippines - Manila Campus" received an overall mean of 5.00 with a rating of Excellent as shown in Table 2.

**Table 2: Summary of Respondents Rating (n=10)**

| Criteria | Mean | Descriptive Rating |
|---|---|---|
| Functionality Suitability | 5.00 | Excellent |
| Performance Efficiency | 5.00 | Excellent |
| Compatibility | 5.00 | Excellent |
| Usability | 5.00 | Excellent |
| Security | 5.00 | Excellent |

1. Functionality Suitability

Functionality Suitability refers to the extent to which the system performs its intended tasks and features correctly. In the case of "TechClinic: A Health Record and Analytics System," the system received a mean score of 5.00, indicating that respondents found all core functions fully operational and effective. This includes features such as patient record management, diagnosis processing, medicine inventory tracking, analytics reporting, and notification alerts, all of which were successfully executed without any critical issues. The excellent rating signifies that the system consistently met user expectations in terms of performance and utility.

2. Performance Efficiency

Performance Efficiency measures how well the system performs in terms of speed, responsiveness, and resource usage. With a mean rating of 5.00, the TechClinic system demonstrated excellent efficiency, ensuring fast load times and quick response to user commands. The frontend built with React JS and Vite maintained smooth communication with the backend (Node.js/Express), and real-time data updates across modules such as Dashboard metrics, Analytics graphs, and Notification alerts performed without noticeable delays. This level of performance is essential for real-time health data processing and analytics, minimizing delays and maximizing productivity for healthcare providers and administrative staff.

3. Compatibility

Compatibility assesses the system's ability to function correctly across different devices, browsers, and operating environments. The TechClinic system received a mean score of 5.00, confirming that the application performed consistently across major web browsers including Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, and Safari. The responsive design also ensured proper rendering on desktop, tablet, and mobile screen sizes, and the public-facing registration portal displayed correctly on shared kiosk or tablet devices. This excellent rating demonstrates that the system is accessible to users regardless of their preferred platform or device, which is essential for a clinic environment where staff may access the system from different workstations.

4. Usability

Usability pertains to how easy and intuitive the system is for users to operate. The TechClinic system achieved a perfect score of 5.00, suggesting that users experienced minimal to no difficulty in navigating the interface, completing tasks, and learning how to use the system. Respondents confirmed that doctors, nurses, and administrators could efficiently complete their respective tasks such as registering patients, diagnosing conditions, managing inventory, and reviewing analytics. The role-specific sidebar menus, clear form layouts, and intuitive navigation contributed to an overall smooth user experience, which is crucial in health-related platforms.

5. Security

Security evaluates the system's ability to protect sensitive data and enforce proper access controls. With a mean rating of 5.00, the TechClinic system demonstrated excellent security measures through role-based access control (RBAC) for Doctor, Nurse, and Admin roles, JWT token authentication on all protected API endpoints, and Row Level Security (RLS) policies at the database level. Respondents confirmed that patient records, medicine data, and personnel information were accessible only to authorized users, and that the public registration portal did not expose any authenticated data. Input validation and sanitization on all form submissions further ensured protection against injection attacks. This excellent rating confirms that the system meets the necessary standards for handling sensitive health information.
